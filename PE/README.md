3. **Cap table modeling** for a single funding round

## Medium

## Complex

7. **Add-on acquisition (buy-and-build) model**: platform + 3 bolt-ons, combined leverage and synergy realization
8. **Full investment memo**: LBO, market analysis, management assessment, risk factors — real target company
